Customizing Bootstrap with Sass

  1. Open your custom.scss file.
  2. Before importing Bootstrap, override variables: 
    $primary: #6610f2;
$border-radius: 1rem;
@import "../node_modules/bootstrap/scss/bootstrap";
  3. Recompile your SCSS: sass scss/custom.scss css/custom.css
  4. Reload your HTML to see the new primary color and border radius.
